How to Play

Eggy Car is an entertaining driving game where you control a car carrying a loose egg on its roof. Your objective is to transport this fragile egg as far as possible over bumpy, hilly roads without letting it fall and break.

Start by carefully driving your car forward. Navigate through the challenging terrain by controlling your acceleration and managing your balance on hills and slopes. Collect coins along the way to unlock new vehicles with different characteristics.

Remember, patience and balance are crucial! Use the controls wisely to prevent the egg from falling as you climb hills or descend slopes. The game ends immediately if the egg falls and breaks.

Game Controls

  • Right Arrow or D: Accelerate the car forward
  • Left Arrow or A: Accelerate the car backward

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main objective of Eggy Car?

The main objective of Eggy Car is to drive your car as far as possible while keeping the egg balanced on top of your vehicle. The game ends when the egg falls off and breaks.

How do I control the car?

Use the right arrow key or D to accelerate forward, and the left arrow key or A to accelerate backward. Careful acceleration and deceleration are key to maintaining the egg's balance.

What can I do with the coins I collect?

Coins can be used to purchase new cars with different designs and handling characteristics. Some vehicles might make it easier to transport the egg over certain terrain types.

Are there any tips for beginners?

For beginners, we recommend taking it slow at first. Don't rush or accelerate too quickly, as this can easily cause the egg to fall. Practice gentle acceleration and deceleration, especially when approaching hills. Observe the road ahead and plan your movements accordingly.

Is Eggy Car free to play?

Yes, Eggy Car is completely free to play online at eggy-car.online. No downloads or purchases are required to enjoy the full game experience.

Can I play Eggy Car on mobile devices?

Yes, Eggy Car is designed to be responsive and can be played on most mobile devices with a modern web browser. On touch screens, you'll have on-screen controls to move your car.

Who created Eggy Car?

Eggy Car was created by Beedo Games and was officially released in February 2019. Since then, it has become a popular casual physics-based driving game.
